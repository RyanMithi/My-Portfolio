 Professional Profile

Ryan is an ICT officer with years of experience but currently working with Central Medical Stores Trust. I began my career with St. John of God Hospitaler Services back in 2012 as an intern. I have worked with various organizations such as Feed The Children International, National Bank of Malawi, Plan International, AKI Investment, and currently with Central Medical Stores Trust.

I finished my Uni in 2012 at the National College of Information Technology, where I earned an International Diploma in Computer Studies. I am currently studying for a degree in Information Communication Technology at Mzuzu University, which I started in 2023. My aspiration is to finish my degree and study for another degree in Data Science.

 Personal Profile

I was born in a small city of Mzuzu which is in the Northern part of Malawi, at St Johns Hospital on the 1st of July 1900. I am the first of the a family of four chidlren, 3 boys and 1 girl. I was born from a christian family and was raised as christian.

I have grown up in the ghetto area of Zolozolo where substance abuse and living close to crime is always an issue especially to youth. My parents were very strict when it came to association with our surrounding to keep us safe from ill behaviour. Basically we would play indoors and most just within the house circles within ourselves as family.
